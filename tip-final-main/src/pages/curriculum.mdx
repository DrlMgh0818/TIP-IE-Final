<h2 class="text-center text-secondary">Curriculum Leading to the Degree B.S. in Industrial Engineering, Effective SY 2018-2019</h2>

The curriculum for the B.S. in Industrial Engineering is designed to integrate the various sciences concerned with technology, production of goods, performance of services, and the efficiency of work processes. It prepares students for a broad spectrum of professional activities, including production planning and control, manufacturing systems and processes, facilities design, and more.
