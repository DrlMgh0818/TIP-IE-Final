The College of Industrial Engineering invites you to participate in our upcoming Seminar Series, titled "Innovations in Process Optimization". This event will feature leading experts in the field discussing the latest research findings, technological advancements, and practical applications to enhance efficiency and productivity in industrial processes.

## Seminar Highlights

- **Keynote Speech**: Dr. Elena Roberts will explore the integration of AI and machine learning in optimizing manufacturing workflows.
- **Panel Discussion**: Industry leaders and academic scholars will debate the future directions of process optimization technologies.
- **Workshops**: Hands-on sessions on applying Lean Six Sigma methodologies for process improvement.

Join us for this enriching experience, designed to foster collaboration and innovation among professionals and students alike. Whether you're a seasoned engineer or just starting your journey in industrial engineering, this seminar series promises valuable insights and networking opportunities.

### Event Details

- **Date**: April 15 - 20, 2024
- **Location**: Industrial Engineering College Auditorium and online platforms
- **Registration**: Free for students and faculty members. Please register [here](#).

Don't miss this opportunity to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving field of industrial engineering. We look forward to welcoming you to our seminar series!
